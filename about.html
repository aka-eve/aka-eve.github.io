More About Me.

I'm sangyeop. I obtained B.S.(2021) degree from Electrical and Computer Engineering at Ajou university. Currently, I'm a M.S. & Ph.D. student at Laboratory of Advanced Imaging Technology (LAIT) in the Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology (UNIST), under the supervision of Prof.Jaejun Yoo.

My main research area lies at the intersection of generative models, optimization, and machine learning. I am particularly interested in personalized generative models. My research focuses on computationally efficient and data-efficient generative models to address the cost issues that pose significant barriers for individuals. Additionally, I have a strong interest in image generation and editing using generative models such as diffusion models and GANs, as well as in methods for evaluating these models.
